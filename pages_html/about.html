Valhöll

Eirik's last heartbeat on a blood-slick deck births a legend as a Valkyrie's silver wings pluck him from the waves, only for him to awaken in a Valhalla of empty halls and smoldering braziers — Odin has vanished and the realm's sacred order unravels. Armed with whispered fragments of broken runes and the Valkyrie Brynja's unwavering faith, he must journey through echoing voids where fallen heroes wander, navigate overgrown gardens of cursed steel, and descend into the fiery crucible of the Fire Realm to face the fate that binds the Nine Worlds.

When ancient wards lie shattered and the throne stands vacant, what single ember of courage can reignite the storm that holds reality together?
